About Me

My Vision

The less problems in front of us, the more solutions we can discover. This philosophy tenaciously guides my vision as a software developer: to make the life of myself and those around me less difficult than it needs to be. Each removed problem from one's life creates time and resources to dedicate to something else equally or more worthwhile. I strive to propogate these beliefs through much of my work as an engineer.
